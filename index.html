About Us


KGISL Institute Of Technology.....//.....

KGiSL Institute of Technology (KiTE) was established in the year 2008 under KG Trust by Dr. G. Bakthavathsalam, MS, FICS, FCCP, FAMS, FMMC, Founder-Chairman of KG Hospital, with an objective to offer quality education at the undergraduate and post graduate levels with relevance to industry embedded education, to mold the students for leadership in various fraternities like industry, government & educational institutions; to advance the knowledge base of the engineering professions and to influence the future directions of engineering education and practice.KGiSL Institute of Technology is recognized as an intellectual industry hub for learning and practice being sponsored by KG Information Systems Private Limited.

Department Of Information Technology

A work-focused IT degree allows you to choose from a wide range of career options in software development, computer programming, business analysis, data modeling, systems integration and network management. The future belongs to knowledge based industries. The strength of a country depends on the best use of Information Technology in all walks of life. In the global context, software development and IT-enabled services have emerged as great opportunities for India.

The Symposium serves as an exceptional platform, meticulously crafted to converge the brilliant minds residing in and around our locality. Our mission is to create a dynamic forum where these luminaries can collectively showcase their expertise, igniting a vibrant exchange of knowledge and ideas. This immersive experience acts as a catalyst for emerging talents, providing them with unparalleled access to insights from their peers and mentors alike. Through this collaborative journey, participants gain profound insights into pertinent subjects and their interconnectedness. This symposium promises to be a veritable feast for the intellectually inclined, appealing to the voracious appetite of tech enthusiasts and visionaries. At the helm of this transformative event is the Department of Information Technology, a beacon of innovation and progress. As stalwarts of organization and precision, we orchestrate every facet of this symposium, ensuring an experience that leaves participants invigorated and inspired. Our remarkable infrastructure stands as a testament to our commitment, and our nimble adaptability ensures a seamless experience.
